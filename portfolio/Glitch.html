Living by the beach for the majority of my life made me appreciate just how stunning the sunsets in Southern California can be, so I like to capture the moments whenever I can, filling my camera roll up with sunsets in different locations. I had a really fun time trying out the Audacity program and manipulating the image with the various effects that were offered. I think one of my favorite effects was the “Amplify” effect that added a magical-looking silver filter to the photo, giving it a 3D-like feel to the image almost as if there are layers that extend outward into the image. I named the project, “Multiversal Sunset” because the glitch came out very similar to how the worlds and characters glitch in the movie, “Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse”. If you are not familiar with the movie, it is an animated Spider-Man movie where characters from different universes get sent to another, and since they are not in the correct universe, they glitch out in a quick and sporadic way.
