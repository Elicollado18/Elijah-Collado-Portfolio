A concept that I was interested in basing my memes around was the situations that a majority of us have been in during the recent global pandemic. I feel like memes are more relatable when you know most of your audience has been in the same or similar situations. I created this meme to best convey the feelings of social awkwardness and lonely situations that most people have experienced through the past year and a half. People have endured many emotions throughout the pandemic but I would say that a majority of people have at one point expressed feelings of fear and anxiety.
